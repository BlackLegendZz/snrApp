Calculate your Signal-to-Noise ratio!

This is a simple online calculator for your Signal-to-Noise (SNR) ratio. To do so you have to enter the average brightness of your target and the background of one of your light frames. This is best done in your favourite tool for editing astro-images. The same thing is required for one of your dark and bias frames. The last step is to enter your cameras read noise (which you can find on the manufacturers website) as well as the settings you used for your session.
